ℹ️ How do these sliders work? (click to expand)

Every slider applies a citywide change. Effects combine — e.g. greening + cool roofs both cool, and stack.

Ambient (−5…+5 °C) — adds this offset to every temperature reading and every building's heat color.
🌳 Urban green (0…100%) — cools buildings up to −4°C, air −1.5°C, drops pollution up to −35%, plants up to 220 generic trees in street gaps.
Cool roofs (0…100%) — reflects sunlight back to space. Cools buildings up to −3°C, air −0.8°C. No pollution effect.
Wind (−50…+100%) — boosts/cuts ventilation. Disperses heat (−1.2°C max) and pollution (−30% max). Negative = stagnant, traps everything.
Traffic (0…200%) — scales vehicle emissions. NO₂, NO, CO, soot, noise all scale roughly linearly. 200% = rush hour; 0% = car-free.
Industry (0…200%) — scales industrial emissions. Mostly affects SO₂, NH₃, PM2.5 — different mix than traffic.
🌊 Sea level / surge (0…10 m) — hydrostatic water level above ground. Visually submerges low-elevation buildings.
⚡ Power outage (0…100%) — outage severity. Cascades into traffic/industry effects in some presets.
🔥 Wildfire (0…100%) — wildfire intensity. Boosts O₃ and PM2.5; nudges climate offset up.
🚨 Evacuation routes (0…100%) — prominence of evacuation route overlays.

For localized what-ifs (trees in a single zone, close one street, etc.), use the Predictive Zones toolbar at the bottom of the screen.

ℹ️ What does each scenario do? (click to expand)

These presets change the whole city at once by setting the sliders below. For a localized what-if (e.g. trees in one neighborhood, closing one street), use the Predictive Zones toolbar at the bottom of the screen.

🌳 Greening
🌲 More trees — 75% green coverage citywide. Cools every building by up to −3°C, drops NO₂ ~26%, plants ~165 generic city trees.
🌳 Max greening — 90% green + 60% cool roofs + slight breeze (+10%), under mild warming (+0.5°C). Best-case nature-based scenario.
🔥 Extreme events
🔥 Heatwave '03 — +4°C climate, stagnant air (−30% wind), traffic & industry boosted. Models the 2003 European heatwave (~70 000 deaths). Buildings glow deep red; AC demand spikes a 25% outage.
☁️ Pollution spike — winter inversion: +1.5°C, wind crashes 40%, traffic +80%, industry +70%. NO₂ × ~1.7, PM2.5 climbs sharply.
⛈️ Storm — Black Sea gale: +90% wind, 0.8 m surface flood, 30% outage, traffic drops to 70%.
❄️ Cold snap — −3.5°C, +60% wind, fewer cars. Pollution drops naturally; heating demand spikes.
🌊 Coastal & infrastructure hazards
🌊 Flood +2 m — 2 m water above ground, half of evacuation routes activated, 35% outage. Lower floors of seafront buildings submerged.
🌊 Flood +5 m — catastrophic: most of seaside Codogno underwater, 70% outage, traffic at 30%.
🌀 Storm surge — combined: 3.5 m flood + 95% wind + 75% evacuation. Worst-case Black Sea event.
⚡ Blackout — citywide grid failure during a heatwave (+3.5°C, 80% outage). Traffic surges as people flee, no AC, ignition risk rises.
🔥 Wildfire — +3°C, +50% wind, 85% wildfire intensity. Smoke plume drives PM2.5 and O₃ way up.
🟠 Earthquake — infrastructure collapse: 70% outage, traffic at 50%, industry at 30%, evacuation 70%.
🚨 Evacuation — highlights all evacuation routes at full prominence. Traffic and industry drop sharply.
🏙️ Net-zero pathways
2030 — +1.2°C, 25% greening, 25% cool roofs, traffic & industry down to 80%. Realistic EU 2030 milestone.
2040 — +1.6°C, 45% green, 50% cool roofs, traffic & industry at 55%. Mid-transition.
2050 net-zero — +2°C (locked in), 70% green, 80% cool roofs, traffic at 25%, industry at 30%. Full electrification + greening.
⚙️ Utility
📍 Baseline — turns simulation on with all defaults. Use as a starting reference before tweaking sliders.
↺ Reset — turns simulation off and clears every slider back to zero.
Tip: presets just set the sliders below. After picking one you can still tweak any slider, or combine with the Predictive Zones toolbar to layer a localized intervention on top of the global scenario.
